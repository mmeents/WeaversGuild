## Evaluation Framework for Your Setup
- High margin potential: Low human touch after setup (review/approve only), repeatable outputs, subscription model.
- Graph fit: Multi-agent/supervisor patterns (one desk routes to specialists), stateful loops for refinement, tool integrations (MCP calls).
- Entry barriers: Start narrow (one vertical) to prove the line, then expand.
- Risks: Hallucinations (add verifier desks), copyright on outputs, client data privacy.

Quick wins to test:
- Build the content repurposing line first — it matches the clip/engagement conversation we had earlier and has fast feedback loops.
- Package it as a self-serve tool inside WeaversGuild or offer "done-for-you" runs.
- Validate by running it on your own content or offering cheap pilots on X/Indie Hacker communities.
